Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you respond to an emergency?

We offer 24/7 emergency response services and typically arrive on-site within 60-90 minutes of your call, depending on your location within our service area.

Will my insurance cover restoration services?

Most homeowner's insurance policies cover sudden and accidental water damage, fire damage, and other disasters. We work directly with all major insurance companies to streamline the claims process and minimize your out-of-pocket expenses.

How long does the restoration process take?

The timeline varies depending on the extent of the damage. A simple water extraction might take 3-5 days for drying, while a comprehensive fire restoration could take several weeks. We provide a detailed timeline after our initial assessment.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes, we provide free, no-obligation estimates for all our restoration services. Our technicians will thoroughly assess the damage and provide a transparent, detailed breakdown of the costs before any work begins.
